Commercial Space Flight

To see the earth from space is the dream of many people. SpaceX has announced that it will open up the first commercial space craft that will make it possible for people to have the experience of watching the earth from space. This wouldn't be possible without the clearence of the U.S government which has accelerated the boom of the space industry. The expected year this will be open to the public will be in the year 2030.

Exciting times

There are exciting times happening on the U.S. The governments focus on space technology has not been dismissed. The people of the World are excited about what will happen in the next coming year. It seems like the race for the space has begun and will be starting to accelerate in the upcoming years.
