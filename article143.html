International talents can now work in SpaceX

The U.S government has now lessen the restriction for international to work for SpaceX to further the growth of the exciting Space Industry that is happening in America. Further more this will benefit SpaceX greatly because of the vast pool of talented people all around the world, that has now the oppurtunity to work for SpaceX will bring the world more closer together. Even though the recruitment process only has allowed U.S citizens to work for the space company in the recent years, Elon Musk says that this will benefit both the U.S and other international countries around the world.

Recruitment Program

The recruitment program will be starting at the end of 2025 where it will be expected to begin in India. The recent involvement of Jeff Bezos founder of Amazon and Blue origin in India made it clear to Elon Musk that India must be one of that starting point of the recruitment process because of the vast talent for engineers in the region. India has a population of over 1 billion people and it would be great oppurtunity to strengthen the relations between SpaceX and the people of India.
